Meet out Founder, Becca

A lifelong love for music

Becca, known online as BeccasMelodies, has always had a deep connection to music. From the first time she picked up a guitar, she was captivated by how each chord and note could tell its own story. What began as a passion for playing quickly became a desire to share that joy with others.
Through her social media channels, Becca's accessible, down-to-earth guitar tutorials have helped countless beginners start their musical journeys. She breaks down techniques in a way that's easy to follow, focusing on building confidence and enjoying the process. Her goal is To make guitar accessible to everyone, no matter their background or experience level.
