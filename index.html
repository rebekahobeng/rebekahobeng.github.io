Tips For Curly Hair...

  • Always comb your hair from the bottom of your hair and work your way up.
  • Trim regulary to avoid split ends and unhealthy hair.
  • You should rely most on wide-tooth combs - never a brush detangler, because you would end up damaging and possibly losing hair.
  • Always apply conditioner to the ends of your hair.
  • Don't use your shampoo too often because even though you are cleaning it, you can take lots of moisture out of your hair.

Tips For Straight hair...

  • Make sure to find the RIGHT conditioner for your hair.
  • Don't overuse a product becuase straight hair can only hold so much.
  • Gurad your hair from statis as much as you can!
  • Keep your styling kind of simple becuase your hair isn't going to be able to hold really tight hairstyles.
  • Avoid brushing wet hair as it may cause hair to stretch or break off.
